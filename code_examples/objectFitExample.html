Object-fit method

- Use the object-fit property for your responsive images.

In this example, 2 values are specified - cover and contain.

container - The replaced content is scaled to maintain its aspect ratio while fitting within the element’s content box.

cover - The replaced content is sized to maintain its aspect ratio while filling the element’s entire content box. If the object's aspect ratio does not match the aspect ratio of its box, then the object will be clipped to fit.
